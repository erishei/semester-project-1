Accessibility:

Test

  • Wheelchair accessibility with ramps and elevators
  • Accessible parking spaces
  • Braille signage and tactile maps for visually impaired visitors
  • Audio descriptions or guided tours for visually impaired visitors
  • Captioned videos or transcripts for hearing impaired visitors
  • Assistive listening devices for hearing impaired visitors
  • Accessible restrooms and facilities
  • Seating areas or benches for visitors with mobility issues

Facilities:

  • Interactive exhibits and hands-on activities
  • Educational workshops and programs for pre-teens
  • Age-appropriate signage and labels
  • Safety measures and childproofing
  • Seating areas or benches for visitors with mobility issues
  • Wheelchair accessibility with ramps and elevators
  • Accessible restrooms and facilities
  • Healthy food options at the cafeteria

Education:

We offer customizable tours around the museum for schools and kindergartens. These are available Mondays and Wednesdays from 10-12. Please fill out the form on our contacts page to book a time for your group.
